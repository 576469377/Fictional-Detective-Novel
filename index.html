Synopsis | 故事简介

English: Detective Sarah Chen finds herself entangled in a web of mysteries when a series of coded messages leads to unexplained disappearances in the heart of the city. With time running out and the cryptic clues becoming increasingly personal, she must decode the crimson cipher before becoming the next victim.
